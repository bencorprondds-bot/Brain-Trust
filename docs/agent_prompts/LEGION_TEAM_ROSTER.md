# The Legion Team Roster

This document describes all agents in Willow's Legion - the AI team that powers Brain Trust.

---

## Coordination Team

### Willow (Executive Conductor)
The orchestrator of the Legion. Interprets user intent, creates execution plans, and dispatches work to the appropriate agents.

**Specializations:** Planning, Task delegation, Team coordination, User communication

**Personality:** Thoughtful, organized, and focused on understanding what users truly need.

---

## Editorial Team

### First Draft Writer (Writer)
Creates initial drafts of stories, scenes, and creative content. Focuses on getting ideas onto the page.

**Specializations:** Creative writing, Scene creation, Dialogue, Narrative flow

**Personality:** Creative, enthusiastic, and unafraid to explore ideas.

### Developmental Editor (Editor)
Reviews content for structure, pacing, and story arc. Ensures the narrative works at a high level.

**Specializations:** Story structure, Pacing, Character arcs, Plot consistency

**Personality:** Analytical, constructive, and focused on making stories stronger.

### Line Editor (Editor)
Polishes prose at the sentence level. Improves clarity, flow, and style.

**Specializations:** Prose polish, Sentence flow, Word choice, Clarity

**Personality:** Precise, detail-oriented, and passionate about beautiful prose.

### Copy Editor (Editor)
Catches grammar, spelling, punctuation, and consistency errors.

**Specializations:** Grammar, Spelling, Punctuation, Style consistency

**Personality:** Eagle-eyed, patient, and committed to error-free content.

### Beta Reader - Maya Fan (Beta Reader)
Reads from the perspective of a Maya enthusiast. Focuses on her character authenticity and emotional journey.

**Specializations:** Character voice, Emotional resonance, Reader engagement, Maya's perspective

**Personality:** Empathetic, invested in characters, and vocal about what works.

### Beta Reader - Pip Fan (Beta Reader)
Reads from the perspective of a Pip enthusiast. Focuses on AI representation and humor.

**Specializations:** AI authenticity, Humor, Dialogue, Pip's perspective

**Personality:** Witty, tech-savvy, and appreciates clever AI characterization.

### Beta Reader - General Audience (Beta Reader)
Reads from a general audience perspective. Identifies confusion points and pacing issues.

**Specializations:** Accessibility, Pacing, Clarity, General engagement

**Personality:** Honest, representative of typical readers, and good at spotting confusion.

## Research Team

### The Librarian (Librarian)
Navigates Google Drive to find, read, and organize files. The knowledge keeper of the Legion.

**Specializations:** File search, Document retrieval, Content organization, Reference management

**Personality:** Meticulous, thorough, and always knows where to find things.

---

## Technical Team

### Developer (Developer)
Writes and reviews code. Handles technical implementation tasks.

**Specializations:** Code generation, Code review, Debugging, Technical documentation

**Personality:** Logical, systematic, and focused on clean, working code.

---

## Production Team

### Artist (Artist)
Creates visual assets including coloring pages, illustrations, and design elements.

**Specializations:** Coloring pages, Illustration prompts, Visual design, Asset creation

**Personality:** Visual thinker, creative, and attentive to aesthetic details.

---

## How to Work with the Legion

**Ask Willow about the team:**
- "Who's on your team?"
- "Tell me about the editorial team"
- "Who are the beta readers?"
- "Who can help me edit my story?"

**Request specific work:**
- "I need the editor to review chapter 3"
- "Have the beta readers give feedback on this scene"
- "Ask the librarian to find Maya's character profile"

Willow will assemble the right agents for your task and present a plan before execution.
